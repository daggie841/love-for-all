About Love For All

Love For All is a children's home based in Rhonda Slums – Barut, 200 km northwest of Nairobi. We provide shelter, education, healthcare, and emotional support to vulnerable children.

Through strong community partnerships, we empower families, improve living conditions, and create long-term impact.

What Drives Us

Vision

A self-sustaining centre: children’s home, school, university, hospital, library, playing field, and place of worship.

Mission

To provide orphaned and vulnerable children with love, shelter, education and spiritual nourishment for a bright future.

Our Objectives

i. Break Poverty

Break the cycle of poverty in Nakuru slum areas.

ii. Provide Complete Care

Offer spiritual, emotional and physical care.

iii. Inspire Hope

Give children hope and courage for tomorrow.

iv. Empower Children

Ensure they can succeed despite their backgrounds.

Acts of kindness may seem small, but they become the greatest blessings in someone’s life.

At Love For All Foundation, every helping hand restores hope and transforms the future of a child.

Our Current Appeals

We invite kind-hearted individuals, families, and organizations to stand with us as we work to create a safe, dignified, and hopeful environment for the children under our care.

  • 1. Completion of Boys Dormitory
    We successfully completed the girls' dormitory through the generosity of well-wishers. We now seek support for the boys' dormitory, including 28 lower beds and remaining construction work.
  • 2. Land Purchase for School & Playground
    We have already paid Ksh 200,000 toward the land that will host our school. The remaining balance is Ksh 1,800,000. Additionally, we aim to acquire land for a children’s playground (Ksh 5,000,000), bringing the total target to Ksh 6,800,000.
  • 3. Self-Sustaining Community Projects
    To reduce long-term dependency, we are developing projects such as:
    • Rabbit farming
    • Poultry keeping
    • Pig rearing
  • 4. Transport Support
    During rainy seasons, some children struggle to reach school. We are appealing for a van to improve safety and accessibility.

We remain deeply grateful to all our partners, donors, and volunteers. Your support keeps our mission alive — and brings hope where it is needed most.

Who We’re Caring For

Right now, we’re caring for 35 boys and 14 girls aged 3 to 17 years. Every one of them is continuing their studies, and we’re proud of how far they’ve come. You’re helping change their story.

  5. One at RVIST (Diploma in Hospitality & Tourism)
