Love For All Foundation

Giving hope. Building futures. Supporting vulnerable children in Rhonda Slums, Barut.

For years, Love For All Foundation has taken in abandoned, orphaned, and at-risk children—offering them safety, education, love, and a chance to dream again. Our mission is to transform young lives and break cycles of poverty through sustainable support systems, mentorship, and community empowerment.

Every contribution helps us expand our shelter, complete the boys’ dormitory, support education, and secure land for a safe playground. Together, we can rebuild destinies and restore lost hope.

About Love For All

Love For All is a children's home located in Rhonda Slums – Barut, 200 km northwest of Nairobi. We offer shelter, education, healthcare, and emotional support to vulnerable children. Our program is fully registered and recognized by the Government of Kenya, ensuring transparency, accountability, and adherence to national child welfare standards.

In partnership with our community and supporters, we work to uplift families, improve living conditions, and create sustainable, long-term impact.

What Drives Us

Vision

A self-sustaining centre: children’s home, school, university, hospital, library, playing field, and place of worship.

Mission

To provide orphaned and vulnerable children with love, shelter, education and spiritual nourishment for a bright future.

Our Current Appeals

We invite kind-hearted individuals, families, and organizations to stand with us as we work to create a safe, dignified, and hopeful environment for the children under our care.

  • 1. Completion of Boys Dormitory
    We completed the girls' dormitory through generous support. We now seek help for the boys’ dormitory, including 28 lower beds and finishing work.
  • 2. Land Purchase for School & Playground
    We already paid Ksh 200,000 toward school land. Remaining balance: Ksh 1,800,000. Playground land target: Ksh 5,000,000.
  • 3. Self-Sustaining Community Projects
    We are developing long-term empowerment initiatives:
    • Rabbit farming
    • Poultry keeping
    • Pig rearing
  • 4. Transport Support
    During rainy seasons, children struggle to reach school. We appeal for a van to improve safety and accessibility.

We remain deeply grateful to all our partners, donors, and volunteers. Your support keeps our mission alive — and brings hope where it is needed most.

Who We’re Caring For

Right now, we’re caring for 35 boys and 14 girls aged 3 to 17 years. Every one of them is continuing their studies, and we’re proud of how far they’ve come. You’re helping change their story.
