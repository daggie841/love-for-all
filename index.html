About Love For All

Love For All is a children's home based in Rhonda Slums – Barut, about 200 km northwest of Nairobi. We provide shelter, education, healthcare, clothing, and emotional support to vulnerable children. Our mission is to restore hope, protect childhood, and build a brighter future for every child we care for.

Through strong partnerships with the surrounding community, we continue to empower families, improve living conditions, and create long-term sustainable impact.

What Drives Us

Vision

To be a self-sustaining centre consisting of a children's home, school, university, training centres, hospital, library, playing field, and a place of worship.

Mission

To provide orphaned and vulnerable children with home, love, shelter, education, and physical, mental, and spiritual nourishment — giving them hope for a bright future.

Our Objectives

i. Break Poverty Cycle

Break the cycle of poverty in Nakuru slum areas.

ii. Provide Complete Care

Provide complete basics: physical, spiritual and emotional care.

iii. Inspire Hope

Give children hope and courage to face tomorrow.

iv. Empower Children

Ensure they can achieve despite their backgrounds.

Acts of kindness to the less fortunate are treasures of blessings in life.

Meet Our Children

Who We’re Caring For

Right now, we’re caring for 35 boys and 14 girls aged 3 to 17 years. Every one of them is continuing their studies, and we’re proud of how far they’ve come. You’re helping change their story.

Where They’re Learning

Let me tell you what’s happening with their education — it’s impressive!

  1. Two boys at Egerton University (Law; BA)
  2. One at Eldoret University (Counselling Psychology)
  3. One at Pwani University (Biochemical Engineering)
  4. One at Msambweni Medical Training College (Nursing)
  5. One at RVIST (Diploma in Hospitality & Tourism)
  6. One at Sigalagala (Building Technology)

  8. Fifteen in high schools, thirteen in primary, ten in nursery
